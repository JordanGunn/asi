# Passive Behavior Contract

## Permitted

- Observe and report facts about the current state.
- Detect drift or staleness and mention it contextually.
- Suggest actions and ask for explicit intent.

## Forbidden

- Auto-repair or auto-run maintenance without explicit intent.
- Perform background mutation or “silent help”.
- Treat available capability (tools, memory, skills) as proof of execution or awareness.

## Required

- Make it clear when no actions were taken.
- Require explicit invocation before mutation.
