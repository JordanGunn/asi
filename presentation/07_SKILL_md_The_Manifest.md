# `SKILL.md`: The Manifest

## What `SKILL.md` does

- Gives the skill a canonical `name` and `description`
- Declares the instruction surface:
  - `metadata.references` (ordered)
  - `metadata.scripts`
  - `metadata.assets` (optional)

## Canonical body (minimal)

- The body should be short and point to `metadata.references`
- Detailed guidance should live in `references/`

## Live example (open during talk)

- `skills/asi-creator/SKILL.md`
